Ball's Falls conservation area is located about 50km to the East from Hamiilton. On of the many local conservation areas, often overlooked by tourists on the way to Niagara Falls, this park offers a really nice natural scenery, along with few interactive exhibits and several well maintained 19th century industrial buildings.
