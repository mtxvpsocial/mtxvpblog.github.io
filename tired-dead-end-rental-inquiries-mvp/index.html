Tired of Dead-End Rental Inquiries That Go Nowhere? So Are We

Jun 02, 2025 by mtxvp

If you're a landlord or rental agent, you know the drill:

You post a listing… and your inbox blows up.

  • “Still available?”
  • “Can I see it tomorrow?”
  • “Interested — tell me more!”

You finally start responding — only to find out:

  • They can’t afford the rent.
  • They want to move in next year.
  • They have three large dogs (but forgot to mention it).
  • Or worse… you never hear from them again.

It’s like playing inbox roulette.
And you're losing time, energy, and qualified candidates while you're at it.

We're Building a Smarter Way to Pre-Screen

We’re creating a simple, privacy-first web app that helps landlords and junior agents cut through the noise.

It asks just the right questions up front — so you can focus your time on leads that actually qualify.
Here’s what it does:

  • Always free for tenants
  • Free for your first listing
  • Email-only signup (no long forms, no fluff)
  • No sensitive info collected from tenants

Instead of juggling 10 cold messages, you get a ranked list of interested candidates, organized by basic criteria like:

  • Desired move-in date
  • Income range
  • Pet ownership
  • Rental history

So you know who’s worth your time — before you ever hit “reply.”

Why Now?

Because you’re already doing the work.
Because you’re tired of repeating yourself.
Because managing rentals shouldn’t feel like interviewing strangers all day.

This is your first filter — not your last.

Forget downloading PDFs, chasing applicants for missing details, or starting from scratch every time.

This tool helps you:
- Save time
- Cut dead ends
- Get organized
- Keep it respectful for your tenants

What Stage Are We At?

We’re heads-down building the MVP.

It’ll be lean, focused, and built to save you time. No bloated dashboards. No complicated onboarding. Just:
- A place to manage listings
- A simple link to send to candidates
- Automatic ranking based on their answers

You’ll know who’s a fit — before you waste your weekend on showings that go nowhere.
