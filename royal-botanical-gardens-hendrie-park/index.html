Royal Botanical Gardens - Hendrie Park

Apr 01, 2024  |  mtxvp

rbgcanada botanicalgarden daytrips burlington hamilton ontario canada photoes

Situated between Burlington and Hamilton, ON Royal Botanical Gardens is a home to several interconnected natural spaces. You can easily spend full day here, walking amongst exquisitely maintained plants and landscaped areas.

View Larger Map

The whole park is really big, so especially on a hot day you would want to take is slow and explore one park at a time to really relax and take in all that these gardens have to offer.

Hendrie Park is the largest cultivated garden area sitting on 9 hectares, illustrating the diversity of both plants and garden design. There are over 55,000 individual plants of 1,400 types.
