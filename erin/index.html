If you come to visit Erin on a nice summer day:

View Larger Map

you most likely will find yourself somewhere along the Wellington St.

You don't want to miss a charming McMullan Park, where in 2017 the this fiberglass horse "Past Future 2412" was a subject of a heist - residents woke up to find out that it is sitting on the roof of the local high school. Its being a bit shy in this picture:

Another street clock

Erin also a home to mid-summer Erin RAM Rodeo. If you solve the puzzle of finding the parking behind the Erin Agricultural Society building (hint: the entrance is NOT from Wellington St.), you'll be privy to a spectacular show.

Watching young men and women riding those beautiful bulls and horses, you are left wondering if their backbone is made of steel springs - no normal human can take a fall from an anxious beast!
