Scenic Caves

Mar 10, 2024 by mtxvp

Scenic Caves is a beautiful hiking and adventure area in Collingwood, adjacent to Blue Mountains. Make sure to check out their rates, as well as FAQ before heading there as they do have restrictions on shoes (Closed-toed and closed-back running shoes or hiking boots are mandatory) and "No Pets Policy" applies.

View Larger Map

There are numerous trails in this place, winding up and down without end. Bring lots of water with you in summer, and take rests often.

See if you can spot all the wooden figures in the trails

One of the trails will bring you to the caves

You have a choice here. You can either go down the stairs and explore the caves:

Or stay up and explore them from above, while walking through the forest

Another trail will bring you to the suspension bridge:

Bridge is suspended from very thick steel cables - you likely won't be able to wrap your hand around it.

View at the tree tops from between bridge walkway

Views from the bridge are unreal
