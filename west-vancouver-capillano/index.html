Capilano Suspension Bridge Park in West Vancouver

Mar 25, 2024 by mtxvp

Caption

One of the most unusual hikinng places in Canada, Capilano Suspension Bridge Park is a full day experience if you find yourself visiting Vancouver area in BC. Treetops Adventure of Capilano park is a luxury and relaxed version of Treetop Trekking. Here you have wide, stable and convenient walkways high up in the trees, where you can enjoy the view of the forest from over 30 meters above the ground.
